AI Red Teaming

Adversarial testing of your AI systems before attackers find the weaknesses. I simulate real-world attacks to identify vulnerabilities in your LLM deployments, chatbots, and AI-powered applications.

What's Included

  • Prompt Injection Testing - Direct and indirect injection attacks to bypass system prompts and access controls
  • Jailbreak Assessment - Testing model guardrails against known and novel bypass techniques
  • Data Extraction Attempts - Probing for training data leakage, system prompt extraction, and PII exposure
  • Safety Bypass Testing - Evaluating content filters and safety mechanisms under adversarial pressure
  • Integration Vulnerability Review - Testing how AI components interact with your broader system architecture

Deliverables

  • Comprehensive vulnerability report with severity ratings
  • Proof-of-concept demonstrations for each finding
  • Prioritized remediation recommendations
  • Executive summary for non-technical stakeholders
  • Follow-up testing to verify fixes
